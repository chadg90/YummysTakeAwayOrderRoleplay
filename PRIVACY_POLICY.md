# Privacy Policy for Yummys Takeaway Order Roleplay

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Yummys Takeaway Order Roleplay ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to your privacy. **We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.** Specifically, we do NOT collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Payment information
- Any data that leaves your device

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following information **only on your device** and **never transmits it anywhere**:

- **Game Preferences**: Your selected restaurant type, voice preferences, game settings (order time, custom menu items)
- **Age Verification**: Birth year input (used only to verify age 18+ for settings access, stored locally)
- **Game State**: Current game progress and menu selections (temporary, reset when app closes)

All this information is stored locally using iOS's standard storage mechanisms and is never shared, transmitted, or accessed by us or any third parties.

## How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to use, share, or sell. All game data and preferences remain on your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Apple System Services

The app uses the following Apple-provided system services:

- **AVFoundation**: For text-to-speech functionality using iOS system voices
- **AudioToolbox**: For playing system alert sounds and ringtones
- **SwiftUI**: For the user interface

These services are provided by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policies. We do not have access to any data processed by these services.

### Audio Files

The app includes royalty-free audio files (phone ring and doorbell sounds) that are bundled with the app and played locally. These files do not transmit any data.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no data to secure. All information stored locally on your device is protected by iOS's built-in security features.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be family-friendly and can be used by children under adult supervision. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. All data remains on the device and is never transmitted.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All game preferences and settings can be changed or reset directly within the app.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
